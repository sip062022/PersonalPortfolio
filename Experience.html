Professional Experience

Honeywell Aerospace Technologies
From June to August of 2024, I interned at Honeywell Aerospace Technologies as an integrated supply chain inntern. In this role, I worked alongside the materials management team to improve inventory accuracy and standard operating procedures through the use of SAP and Excel. Over the course of my internship, I was able to create annual cost savings of roughly $50,000 and improve overall stockroom accuracy from 81% to 91% through implementing stockroom controls. I also assisted with the selloff of a $4 million product line of over 2.6 million units, during which I assisted with ensuring systematic accuracy for the last shipment of the product line to the customer. Finally, during our yearly inventory, my team and I were able to obtain 98% inventory accuracy with less than $1 million in discrepencies.
Supply Chain Professionals at USF
For the past two years, I have been involved with Supply Chain Professionals at USF, a student-led professional organization which supports supply chain management networking and professional development, since December of 2022. My journey working with the organization began in December of 2022 when I began working as the Vice President of Membership. In this role, I organized promotional tabling events, pitched the organization to groups ranging from 20 to 400 people, streamlined our modes of communication to be on a single platform, and initiated the creation of our monthly newsletter. In December of 2023, I assumed my current role of President, in which I lead a team of 13 students to carry out the functions of the organization and continuously expand our presence on campus. This involves fostering connections with companies which present to our members, organizing corporate tours, and finding new ways to enhance our members' experiences.
